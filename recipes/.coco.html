Hot Coco Recipe

Description

This is the best hot chocolate you will taste in your whole entire life. You will literally not know what to do with your life after you have consumed it. Don't even get me started on the marshmallows that can be added. Marshmallows are expensive though and should only be added if you are rich.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/3 cup of boiling water

Steps

  1. Combine the cocoa, sugar and pinch of salt in a saucepan.
  2. Blend in the boiling water.
  3. Bring this mixture to an easy boil while you stir.
  4. Simmer and stir for about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in 3 1/2 cups of milk and heat until very hot, but do not boil!
  6. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
  7. Divide between 4 mugs. Add the cream to the mugs of cocoa to cool it to drinking temperature.
